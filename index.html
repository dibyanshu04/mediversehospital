Infertility Treatment

Our hospital is renowned for proving excellence in fertility treatment. The problem of infertility includes both males and females partners equally. So we cater the treatment of both male and female problems.

Why should you choose

Healthy Pregnancy Fertility Center?

Pregnancy Check-Up

We give prime most importance to our pregnant patients as pregnancy is a beautiful gift of nature. We handle every patient with full sanitation and hygiene and also maintaining privacy of every patient. We provide the facility of ultrasonography in every visit free of cost.We involve male partners as well so that they get attached to the baby in womb.

Pediatric Check-Up

We have a qualified team for your baby as well. Provide a neat and clean environment for your child. We have facilities of PICU, NICU, ventilators. We also provide bedside phototherapy facilities so that the baby can stay together with the mother even under treatment.

Gynaecology Care

We have a well equipped department of Gynecology which takes care if welfare of the woman in a hollistic manner. We deal with urinary incontinence pre - marital counselling, sexual health, breast care, hygiene related issues of adolescents. We efficiently treat common problems like Leucorrhoea and Irregular Bleeding.

ART (Artificial Reproductive Techniques)

We Provide facilities for IUI with husband and donor semen, Semen Evaluation, Semen Freezing, Embryo Freezing, IVF, Egg Donation, Embryo Donation and Surrogacy.

Endoscopy

Our skilled team provides the facility of Hysteroscopy, laparoscopy for infertility and general surgery cases. The team aims at providing the hollistic well being of the patients.

Laboratory Facilities

We Provide Facilities for all blood and urine tests in the hospital premises. Semen Evaluation is also done here.

General Surgery

We comprises of skilled and experienced surgeons who dedicatedly follow the guidelines of WHO to perform their surgeries. We use the latest medical equipments for our operative procedures through cutting edge surgical care. We provide comprehensive after care consultation to all our patients from OPD, Emergency and admissions.

Cancer Screening

Genital tract cancers are one not the leading causes of female deaths in India. So it is very important to Diagnose it in early stage. So we are here to screen all kinds of Gynecological cancers at our Hospital. We also provide vaccination for young girls and women against cervical cancer.

Family Planning Services

As the saying goes "Family Planning Saves Life" so here we are to offer you a basket of Family Planning options. We provide couple counseling for family planning methods so that you may choose whats's best for you. We offer Pills, Injectable, Cu-T insertion, and tubal ligation both open and laparoscopic

NICU Care / PICU Care

We are the only centre in Gaya with bedded full equipped NICU facility with ventilators. Out doctors are always on their toes to keep your baby safe.
Our NICU is equipped to care for the following problems in babies.
  • Preterm Care
  • Respiratory Distress in Neonate
  • Noeontal Spesis [Injection in the Newborn]
  • Feeding Problems in Newborn

Nutrition

At Motherhood we have the best nutritional is to help you with any breastfeeding issues you face or doubt that you may have regarding nutrition. our specialist can help you with the tips and techniques to ensure your breastfeeding face is hassle-free and successful even if you have had a tough start. our experts will also prescribe supplements that will beneficial and help you with your babies diet as well as for better Nourishment.
