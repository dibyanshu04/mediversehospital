Obstetrics Services

The interval from getting a confirmation of pregnancy to the delivery and the first few months after delivering a baby can be daunting for the lady and the family. Although this is a normal process in physiology, there may be complications ranging from physical and psychological to simply logistical. There are many questions to be answered and many fears to be allayed. Often the family finds itself facing situations which turn this life changing event from a moment of joy to a period of anxiety.

Our team of consultants and midwives take care of the situation from day one. Consultant obstetricians are firmly in charge of the process all the way through, but midwives are the team that form the backbone of obstetric practice at freya. Unlike general nurses. they are specially trained to take care of all aspects of pregnancy and provide hand holding support from the first consultation to the post-partum period. They are complimented by our support time of dietitians, yoga and exercise instructors and lactation consultants.

Let’s go natural

The highlight of this care at Freya is the high number of normal vaginal deliveries. Unlike almost any modern healthcare system anywhere in the world, pregnancy in most parts of India ends in a caesarean section. This is both unnecessary and dangerous. Nature has devised processes for childbirth which work quite well generally without any human intervention. There are benefits to both mother and baby in a normal childbirth. Also, caesarean section even with all modern technology at hand is a major surgery and may have significant complications. We at freya believe in making pregnancy and childbirth as natural as possible with the advantage of latest technology to keep a close eye and intervene when needed.

Services in Obstetrics offered at Mediverse Hospital

  • Routine pregnancy care
  • High risk pregnancy care
  • Painless labour
  • Pregnancy YOGA
  • Dietary counselling for the pre and post-delivery periods
  • Sensory Labour room


Gynaecology Services

Problems of female reproductive system form a significant bulk of health problems faced by women. We offer a comprehensive system of health check-up and treatment of gynecological disorders. We provide a complete range of emergency and elective gynaecological services, which includes management of menstrual disturbances, endometriosis and hormone related problems as well various masses and tumors of the reproductive system.

Urogynaecology offers a comprehensive range of investigations and management options for the treatment of female urinary incontinence and pelvic organ prolapse.

Services offered by our Gynecology Department:

  • Most advanced special care unit for premature babies
  • Laparoscopic surgery for gynaecological diseases
  • Hysteroscopic Surgery – Hysteroscopic Myomectomy, Adhesiolysis, Septum Resection, Tubal Cannulation, Endometrial Resection
  • Colposcopy
  • Thermachoice uterine ablation therapy (Non operative treatment for bleeding disorders of uterus)
  • Gynaecological oncology
  • Planned Parenthood services.
